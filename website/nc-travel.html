Travel Information:

As shown in the graphs above North Carolina had a huge amount more monthly flu cases in the 2019-2020 flu season compared to the 2020-2021 season. This could be due to multiple factors all surrounding the recent COVID-19 pandemic. Less people could be getting the flu because they are not interacting with people as much, and when they are they are wearing masks so less of a chance to get any virus or sickness. This showcases that wearing masks and washing your hands more are key in not getting the flu and should be continued even after the COVID-19 pandemic is over.
