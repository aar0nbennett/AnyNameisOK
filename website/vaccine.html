Vaccine Information:

Over the years the amount of people getting vaccinated has gone up. This is a good sign to see as this brings us closer to herd immunity. However currently only 57.1% of people have been vaccinated in our current flu season. We can do better than this as achieving herd immunity to the flu can help to prevent unnecessary deaths due to the flu. In order to increase this number we encourage you to go to the Call to Action tab in order to contact your state governor in order to promote flu vaccination for every person as this will bring us closer to the goal of herd immunity.
