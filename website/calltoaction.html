Call to action

Given how effective COVID-19 measures have been in the 2020-2021 flu season it is worth considering adopting measures such as mask wearing and social distancing in future flu seasons. In order to implement these measures it is imperative that you contact your local governor so that measures can be put in place to reduce the spread of the flu in future seasons. Any changes in the future requires us to raise our voices today.
